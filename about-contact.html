Who We Are

Founded on one idea: a shipment should never outrun its own paperwork.

Global Network Transit started as a small freight-forwarding desk handling China-origin cargo for a handful of importers. The mode of transport has since grown to air, sea, and ground — but the original discipline hasn't changed: one tracking ID, one point of contact, and status updates that are actually current when a client checks them.

Mission

Move cargo with the same predictability regardless of lane, carrier, or border.

Approach

Named account handlers instead of rotating support queues.

Network

Origin agents in China plus destination partners across 118 countries.

Standard

Bonded, documented handling that meets international compliance rules.

2011

Freight desk opens in Newark, NJ

Started with LCL ocean freight for a small group of importers on the China–US East Coast lane.

2015

Air freight & first China office

Opened a Shenzhen pickup office to shorten origin handling time for time-critical cargo.

2019

Ground network added

Brought last-mile ground delivery in-house rather than subcontracting it.

2023

Unified tracking platform launched

Replaced carrier-specific tracking links with a single manifest ID that follows cargo end to end.

2026

118-country network

Now coordinating air, sea, and ground moves across 46 offices worldwide.
